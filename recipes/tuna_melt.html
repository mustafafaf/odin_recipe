Ingredients

  • Half a tuna tin
  • Mayo
  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the tuna and mayo until chunky paste
  2. Butter 2 slices of bread
  3. Add one slice of bread butter face down to cold pan
  4. On top of that add the tuna paste and cheese
  5. Add bread on top of that butter side up
  6. Leave on low heat until crispy brown on bottom, then flip
  7. Take off when both sides are crispy brown and cheese is melted
